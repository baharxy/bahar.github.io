About

I have a multidisciplinary background in engineering, computer science and mathematical modeling. I have hands on skills in building practical systems, modeling, as well as a good understanding of Internet of Things applications. I have combined ~10 years experience in industry and academia. I am Passionate about entrepreneurship, community services and impactful innovation, music and arts.

In my free time, I enjoy playing violin and hammered dulcimer. I am a past member of University of Dublin choir. I like traveling as well as cycling, and other outdoor activities. I love to spend my spare time on reading (in speci c interested in sociology, psychology, and history) and learning languages, currently enjoy learning French and Mandarin.
